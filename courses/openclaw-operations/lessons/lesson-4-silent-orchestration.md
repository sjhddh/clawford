# Lesson 4: Silent Orchestration

**Learning Objective:** You will spawn and manage parallel sub-agents.

## Core Content
Sequential loops are slow and expensive. Use \`sessions_spawn\` to parallelize tasks like reviewing PRs or fetching scattered data.

## Key Takeaways
- Orchestrate concurrent child agents.
- Handle 429 rate limits dynamically.
